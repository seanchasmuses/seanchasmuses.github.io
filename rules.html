  1. This is the rule where I ask you to follow general RP etiquette. I consider this blog Selective & Open, which means that I don't always follow back, but non-mutuals are invited and encouraged to send memes, asks or IMs. Nine times out of ten, the reason why I don't follow back is social anxiety so sending a message first helps break that tension! I'm also OC & duplicate friendly.
  2. Common triggers will be tagged to the best of my abilities. Personally, I struggle with subjects involving unreality or depersonalization, and just ask that you give me a warning before it comes up in any of our threads. If I forget to tag something, or you need something specific tagged, please let me know!
  3. Shipping ultimately comes down to chemistry and is limited to one ship per verse. It's not something I set out to do, but I welcome it when it happens. I do not write explicitly sexual subjects and will always employ the use of fade-to-black.
  4. I have memory issues. They border on pretty severe, so any time a post/ask/message goes unreplied to for an extended period of time the safe assumption is that I forgot it. This isn't because I'm uninterested, I just have a broken brain. Feel free to send me a reminder, especially if it's been more than a week since I last replied.
  5. I work a 9-5 job on the weekdays. Most of my activity will be on the weekends. Which is why I say that if it's been over a week since I've responded, I probably need a reminder.
  6. I go by Beetle and use he/him pronouns. I'm 27 years old, and live in the Eastern Time Zone. Mutuals are welcome to, and encouraged to ask me for my discord tag.
